Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a logo design project typically take? +

Most logo projects take between 2-5 days from initial consultation to final delivery. The timeline depends on the complexity of the project and the number of revision rounds needed.

What's included in your logo design service? +

Each logo project includes: 3 initial concepts, 2 rounds of revisions, final logo in multiple formats (AI, EPS, PNG, JPG, SVG), and a basic style guide with color codes and font recommendations.

Do you provide branding beyond just the logo? +

Yes! I offer complete brand identity packages that include business cards, letterheads, social media templates, and brand guidelines. Let me know your needs and I can create a custom package.

What are your payment terms? +

I require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. Payments can be made via bank transfer or PayPal.

Can I trademark the logo you design for me? +

Absolutely! Once the final payment is received, you'll have full rights to the design and can trademark it.
